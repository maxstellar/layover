About Layover

FAQ

How do I get prizes?

Ship projects to earn 5 miles per hour of work, tracked using Hackatime or Lapse. Miles can be used in the shop to get travel stipends ($1/mile) or other travel-related prizes!

What projects can I make?

Projects should be fun, creative, and high-effort! Although you can submit any project you like, there will be weekly themes, centered around travel.

What are the requirements?

  • Participants must be 13-18 years old and cannot be banned from Hackatime.
  • Projects must be creative and high-effort.
  • Projects cannot make use of ANY AI assets, and AI-generated code must make up less than 25% of the final shipped project.
