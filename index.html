What is Layover?

Layover is a proposed Hack Club YSWS where you can create travel-related projects and receive "miles" in return!
Miles can be used in the shop to claim travel grants, as well as other travel-related prizes.

What are the requirements?

  • Participants must be 13-18 years old and cannot be banned from Hackatime
  • Projects need to be related to travel (i.e. travel/transport game, trip planner, 3D model for travel accessories)
  • Projects cannot make use of ANY AI assets, and AI-generated code must make up less than 25% of the final shipped project.
